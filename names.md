## Naming Guidelines

When choosing a name, consider:

1. **Domain Availability** - Check if .com/.org/.net is available
2. **Trademark** - Ensure no existing trademarks in your jurisdiction
3. **Pronunciation** - Easy to say in English and local African languages
4. **Cultural Sensitivity** - Appropriate across African cultures
5. **Scalability** - Works if expanding beyond Africa
6. **Logo Potential** - Visual elements for branding
7. **Social Media** - Check handle availability on platforms

- **To rebrand:** Consider EduTech Africa, CareerPath Africa, or AfricaTrain Hub
